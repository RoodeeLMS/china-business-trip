## Key Learnings & Recommendations

### 1. Minimalist Packing Works
- 3 HEATTECH sets with laundry > 6 sets without laundry
- 2 business shirts rotated > 4 shirts
- 1 versatile jacket > multiple specialized jackets

### 2. Smart Material Choices
- **Non-Iron shirts:** No wrinkles, no hassle
- **HEATTECH:** Quick-dry, wash at night, dry by morning
- **Miracle Air Pants:** Lightweight, breathable, versatile
- **Fleece Jacket:** Warm, packable, professional enough

### 3. Hotel Facilities
- Hotels have laundry service
- Can wash small items in bathroom
- HEATTECH dries overnight

### 4. Weight Optimization
- Wear heavy items on flight
- Carry laptop separately
- Use hotel toiletries
- Target: 6.7 kg achievable!

### 5. China-Specific Prep
- ‚úÖ VPN setup essential
- ‚úÖ Download offline maps
- ‚úÖ Alipay/WeChat Pay helpful
- ‚úÖ Chinese Yuan cash needed
- ‚úÖ Universal adapter (Type A/I)
